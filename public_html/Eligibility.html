Course Eligibility Information

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

To be eligible for the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science program, the candidate must have completed high school education with a minimum of 50% marks in the science stream. Additionally, candidates may need to appear for an entrance examination.

Bachelor of Arts in English Literature

Eligibility for the Bachelor of Arts in English Literature program requires a minimum of 45% marks in the 10+2 examination. Students from any stream are eligible to apply, but a passion for literature is highly encouraged.

Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

Candidates applying for the Bachelor of Business Administration program should have completed their 10+2 education with a minimum of 55% marks. Additionally, some colleges may conduct a personal interview as part of the selection process.

Bachelor of Engineering in Mechanical Engineering

The Bachelor of Engineering in Mechanical Engineering program requires candidates to have completed high school with a strong background in physics and mathematics. Minimum marks and entrance exam scores may apply.

Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com)

Eligibility for the Bachelor of Commerce program typically requires a minimum of 50% marks in the 10+2 examination. Students from commerce and science streams are eligible.
