Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How do I apply for admission to the college?

A: You can find the admission process and application forms on our official admissions page. Make sure to check the eligibility criteria before applying.

Q: What documents do I need for admission?

A: The required documents for admission include your high school transcripts, identification proof, passport-sized photographs, and any other documents specified in the admission guidelines.

Q: How can I pay my tuition fees?

A: Tuition fees can be paid through online portals, bank transfers, or in-person at the college finance office. Check our payment guidelines for detailed instructions.

Q: Are there scholarships available for students?

A: Yes, we offer scholarships based on merit, financial need, and specific criteria. Explore our scholarship programs and application process on the scholarships page.

Q: How can I reset my college email password?

A: You can reset your college email password through the IT support portal or by contacting our IT department. Follow the instructions provided on the login page for password recovery.
